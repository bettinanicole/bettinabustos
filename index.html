I am currently a lab manager at Wouter Kool's Control and Decision-Making Lab.

Previously I studied Psychology at UT Austin with an emphasis in Cognitive Neuroscience. My main interests meet at the intersection of executive function and memory for goal-directed behavior, specifically, when, how and why certain strategies are nominated over others and subsequently implemented. I am also interested in how to integrate the influence of memory in reinforcement learning models of decision-making.

Additionally, I like thinking about how findings in the field of Cognitive Neuroscience can inform Moral Psychology.
